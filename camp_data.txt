The Bethesda summer tennis camp offers structured, high-energy sessions focusing on tennis, fitness, and friendships. The camp is open to children and teens ages 6 to 18 of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced, with groups organized by ability.
    
The summer tennis camp runs Monday through Friday. Half-day sessions operate from 9 AM to 12 PM and cost $330 per week. Full-day sessions operate from 9 AM to 3 PM and cost $550 per week.
    
For the first part of the summer, specifically the weeks of June 8th through June 18th, the tennis camp is located at Meadowbrook Park Tennis Courts, 6321 Meadowbrook Lane, Chevy Chase, MD 20815.
    
For the remainder of the summer, from June 22nd through August 14th, the tennis camp is located at Westland Middle School Tennis Courts, 5511 Massachusetts Ave, Bethesda, MD 20816.

Standard 5-day camp weeks run on the following dates: June 8-12, June 22-26, July 6-10, July 13-17, July 20-24, July 27-31, August 3-7, and August 10-14. Any weeks not listed are not available for enrollment.

The camp offers two special 4-day holiday weeks that are discounted by 20%. The week of June 15-18 is a 4-day week observing Juneteenth. The week of June 29-July 2 is a 4-day week observing the 4th of July.

For the summer sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp, registration is handled on a week-by-week basis. Parents may not book the entire summer at once; instead, they must sign up for each individual week they wish their child to attend.
 
For the Spring, Winter, and Fall sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp, parents must first identify the age, estimated performance level, and schedule availability of their child. The camp's website provides details on the estimated age range and expected minimum skills for each level.

Before officially joining the Spring, Winter, or Fall sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp, parents should email the camp to schedule a preliminary diagnostic. Campers can arrive 15 minutes early to a designated practice time for a free 15-minute consultation with a coach to determine the best level. The camp does not offer free full drop-in sessions.

Registration for the Spring, Winter, and Fall sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp is handled through the booking platform, CourtReserve. Parents must create an account and book a specific class for the entire season, specifying the level and number of days per week.

For Spring, Winter, and Fall season registrations at StarrMark Tennis Camp, prices are pro-rated based on how much of the season is remaining at the time of sign-up. However, the camp does not offer any pro-ration after 50% of the season has been completed.
