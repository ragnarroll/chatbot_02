What is the summer tennis camp?
StarrMark Academy’s summer tennis camp in Bethesda offers structured, high-energy sessions focusing on tennis, fitness, and friendships. The camp is open to children and teens ages 6 to 18 of all skill levels, from beginners to advanced, with groups organized by ability.

The summer tennis camp runs Monday through Friday. Half-day sessions operate from 9 AM to 12 PM and cost $330 per week. Full-day sessions operate from 9 AM to 3 PM and cost $550 per week.

What time is the summer camp? What dates? Where?
For the first part of the summer, specifically the weeks of June 8th through June 18th, the tennis camp is located at Meadowbrook Park Tennis Courts, 6321 Meadowbrook Lane, Chevy Chase, MD 20815.

For the remainder of the summer, from June 22nd through August 14th, the tennis camp is located at Westland Middle School Tennis Courts, 5511 Massachusetts Ave, Bethesda, MD 20816.

Standard 5-day camp weeks run on the following dates: June 8-12, June 22-26, July 6-10, July 13-17, July 20-24, July 27-31, August 3-7, and August 10-14. Any weeks not listed are not available for enrollment.

The camp offers two special 4-day holiday weeks that are discounted by 20%. The week of June 15-18 is a 4-day week observing Juneteenth. The week of June 29-July 2 is a 4-day week observing the 4th of July.

How can I register myself or my child for the summer camp at StarrMark Academy?
For the summer sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp, registration is handled on a week-by-week basis. Parents may not book the entire summer at once; instead, they must sign up for each individual week they wish their child to attend.

How can I register myself or my child for the Fall, Winter, or Spring sessions at StarrMark Academy? 
For the Spring, Winter, and Fall sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp, parents must first identify the age, estimated performance level, and schedule availability of their child. The camp's website provides details on the estimated age range and expected minimum skills for each level.

Before officially joining the Spring, Winter, or Fall sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp, parents should email the camp to schedule a preliminary diagnostic. Campers can arrive 15 minutes early to a designated practice time for a free 15-minute consultation with a coach to determine the best level. The camp does not offer free full drop-in sessions.

Registration for the Summer, Spring, Winter, and Fall sessions at StarrMark Tennis Camp is handled through the booking platform, CourtReserve. Parents must create an account and book a specific class for the entire season, specifying the level and number of days per week.

What are the policies regarding pro-rating?
For Spring, Winter, and Fall season registrations at StarrMark Tennis Camp, prices are pro-rated based on how much of the season is remaining at the time of sign-up. However, the camp does not offer any pro-ration after 50% of the season has been completed.

Which level does my child fall into? What are the different levels?
At StarrMark Academy, a player's placement level is determined using a combination of their age and demonstrated tennis skills. While age and skill guidelines are provided, the ultimate decision regarding which level a player is assigned to rests entirely with the coaching staff.

The "Twinkles" level at StarrMark Academy is designed for children ages 3 to 7 who are new to tennis or just getting started. This fun-first group uses red balls and focuses on fundamentals like basic strokes, footwork, coordination, and getting the ball into play. The minimum enrollment for Twinkles is 1 day per week.

The "Rising Stars" level at StarrMark Academy is for players ages 7 to 11 who use orange and green dot balls. These players have developed basic strokes and can successfully rally. This level introduces tactical concepts like spin and placement, alongside continued fitness and yoga development, and introduces competitive play through inner-league matches, USTA events, and UTR tournaments. The minimum enrollment for Rising Stars is 2 days per week.

The "Junior Showcase Invitational" level at StarrMark Academy is for players ages 11 to 14 who demonstrate consistency and can vary their shots with spin, depth, and pace. This group acts as a bridge between developmental and competitive play, preparing players for higher-level tournaments by focusing on strong court positioning and point construction. The minimum enrollment for the Junior Showcase Invitational is 2 days per week.

The "Junior Showcase 1" level at StarrMark Academy is specifically designed for high school-aged beginners, generally ages 13 to 18, using standard yellow balls. It provides a structured on-ramp for older players to develop core tennis skills quickly in a peer-appropriate environment. The minimum enrollment for Junior Showcase 1 is 2 days per week.

The "Junior Showcase 2" level at StarrMark Academy is for advanced juniors using standard yellow balls. These players possess high-level consistency and can execute advanced shots like drop shots, lobs, and angles. Training includes regular competitive match play and focuses on strong tactical understanding, including the ability to adjust game plans to counter opponents. The minimum enrollment for Junior Showcase 2 is 3 days per week.

The "Showcase" level is StarrMark Academy's top, elite competitive group. Players at this level have mastered power and consistency as weapons, can vary their strategy and style under pressure, and participate in high-intensity, tournament-focused training. Enrollment in the elite Showcase level requires a direct coach assessment, and the minimum enrollment is 3 days per week.

What equipment is needed for the camp?
At StarrMark Tennis Camp, players are required to bring specific items to every practice to ensure their health, performance, and enjoyment on the court. The mandatory items that must be packed in a player's tennis bag include two tennis racquets, three tennis balls, a water bottle, a sport towel, sunscreen, a baseball hat or visor, tennis shoes with extra socks, a jump rope, ankle and arm resistance bands, and a notebook with a pen or pencil.

In addition to the mandatory practice gear, StarrMark Tennis Camp highly recommends that players pack a few extra items to cultivate preparedness, especially for matches. These highly recommended items include a seasonal change of clothing, slides or flip flops, a mini medical kit with band-aids and tape, hand sanitizer or a mask, extra hair ties, extra tennis grip, tissues, and a healthy snack.

What is the rain policy?
At StarrMark Tennis Camp, practices are typically held even in the case of rain. Practice will only be canceled under very severe weather circumstances. If the coaching staff decides to cancel a practice due to weather, a notification message will be sent at least 2 hours before the scheduled practice time. In the event that a practice is officially rained out and canceled, StarrMark offers make-up practices, which are typically scheduled during the week immediately following the end of the current season.
